About the Deck

The Cursed Tarot of the Damned

Belladonna Nightshade’s Cursed Tarot of the Damned takes the Rider-Waite tradition and corrupts it through Belladonna’s worldview: power, desire, mortality, false virtue, manipulation, appetite, and survival.

The upright cards are not comforting. They are usable. They reveal forces that can be recognized, claimed, manipulated, survived, or turned toward advantage.

The Reversed Cards

The reversed cards are the diseased version of each force: odious, cynical, spiritually poisonous, and dangerous when left unseen.

Here is the power. Here is the lie. Here is what happens if you refuse to see either.
